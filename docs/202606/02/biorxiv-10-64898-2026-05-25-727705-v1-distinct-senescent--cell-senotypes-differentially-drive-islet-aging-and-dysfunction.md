## 摘要
生物衰老极大地影响身体处理葡萄糖的能力，并且是2型糖尿病（T2D）发生和进展的主要风险因素。然而，尽管细胞衰老研究取得了进展并开发了新的抗衰老治疗药物，但人类内分泌胰腺中细胞衰老的异质性及其在正常衰老中的作用仍有待在单细胞水平上阐明。

在这里，我们对来自26名捐赠者（年龄20-80岁）的完整胰腺进行了单细胞分辨率的空间蛋白质组学和转录组学分析，并对来自14名捐赠者（年龄34-69岁）的分散胰岛进行了多重单细胞RNA测序和功能测定。我们根据CDKN1A和CDKN2A的相对表达区分出两个离散的SnC亚群。

CDKN1A衰老细胞（SnCs）表现出β细胞特性丧失、胰岛素分泌受损以及与胰岛免疫浸润增加相关的促炎性SASP。相比之下，CDKN2A SnCs保留了转录特性和功能能力，炎症信号较低。

总之，这些发现揭示了人类胰腺中异质性和功能分化的衰老类型，区分了适应性（CDKN2A）和适应不良（CDKN1A）衰老程序，从而为T2D的衰老靶向治疗提供了一个基于机制的框架。

## Abstract
Biological aging greatly impacts the bodys ability to handle glucose, and represents a major risk factor the development and progression of type 2 diabetes (T2D). Nonetheless, despite advances in cellular senescence research and the development of new senolytic therapies, the heterogeneity of cellular senescence in the human endocrine pancreas, as well as its roles in normal aging, remains to be elucidated at the single-cell level.

Here, we performed single-cell-resolved spatial proteomics and transcriptomics on intact pancreas from 26 donors (ages 20-80) and multiplexed single-cell RNA sequencing and functional assays on dispersed islets from 14 donors (ages 34-69). We identify two discrete SnC subpopulations distinguished by relative expression of CDKN1A and CDKN2A.

CDKN1A senescent cells (SnCs) exhibit loss of {beta}-cell identity, impaired insulin secretion, and a proinflammatory SASP associated with increased islet immune infiltration. In contrast, CDKN2A SnCs retain transcriptional identity and functional competence, with lower inflammatory signaling.

Together, these findings identify heterogeneous and functionally divergent senotypes in the human pancreas, distinguishing an adaptive (CDKN2A) from a maladaptive (CDKN1A) senescence program, thus providing a mechanism-guided framework for senescence-targeted therapies in T2D.
